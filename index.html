Projects

Real Estate Commission Tracking System

An ERP system that allows Real Estate Companies track commission earnings, by allowing agents to perform data entry operations to add certain informations, such as Contracts, Developers, Locations, Source, and etc. it also allows them to view the commission earning of each agent in the form of charts and graphs

Accounting ERP System

An accounting ERP, that allows companies to manage their variable/fixed expenses, and enter the salary amount of every employee that will be added to the final amount due every month. This ERP also allows that generation of invoices from transactions.

AI Tutor - Personalized Instructor

A Personalized Tutor made using GPT4 technology, that allows students to have one on one tutoring sessions as well as educational video generation using Invideo AI.

Plant Monitoring & Disease Classification APP

An Application that allows user to monitor their plants status such as humidity, moisture, and temperature. The measurements will be taken from sensors that are connected to a raspberry pi. Furthermore the user can examine the condition of the plant using AI (Gemini) to get notified if the plant condition is dangerous. The user can also take a picture of the plant to identify the type of disease it has.

MikroPong - A Pong Game Made With a Microcontroller

In this project we used a Microcontroller to make a pong game that allows up to 2 players, It features 2 7-segment displays for showing the score, 4 buttons for the paddle movements, and a piezo buzzer that makes a noise everytime the ball hits the wall or the goal.
